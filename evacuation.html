what should I do during an earthquake?

source

Before an earthquake (in general)

Prepare Items

  • drinking water
  • dried and canned food
  • first aid kit
  • flashlight
  • portable radio (with spare batteries)

Familiarize yourself with emergency exits

Place heavy objects on the floor or lower lying places

During an earthquake

  • cover head with pillow
  • hide under desk
  • go next to corner of a wall / strong doorframe
  • windows, glass objects, ceiling items (fans, lights, etc)
  • Turn off electricity and gas
